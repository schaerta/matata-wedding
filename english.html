The Big Day

We will celebrate our wedding in Lübars, a wonderful district in the north of Berlin.

Please join us at 1 PM on the grounds of the Alte Fasanerie for our wedding ceremony (details below). In the evening we will celebrate in the historic LabSaal, which is about a half hour walk from the Alte Fasanerie.

Both venues are accessible by public transportation and we will arrange a shuttle for transportation to the LabSaal. So if you would like, you could leave your car at home.

Accommodation

You can of course choose where you want to stay in Berlin. There is an almost endless variety of possibilities. Just keep in mind that the venues are very far north and the distances in Berlin can be very long.

The bus from LabSaal goes to the Waidmannslust S-Bahn stop (line S1) and Tegel (line S25), among other places. So an accommodation that is located at the S1 or in or around Tegel could be well suited.

Our recommendations:
